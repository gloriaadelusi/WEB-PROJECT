What Goes Into Making The Shake that Shakes Your World?

<<<<<<< HEAD

Our milkshakes are made with the environment in mind. We use materials that are 100% recyclable (so you dont have to worry about your milkshake bottles damaging sealife) As an incentive to encourage our customers to recycle, you get 10% on any full priced product if you present 5 empty bottles. 10 empty bottles results in getting 20% off a complete order, sale items and deals of the week.

Our cows are fed with the highest quality of grass and drink water sourced from the Himalayas and there is no animal cruelty. You can be sure that the cows used in the milk in your milkshake are being treated well.

Our dairy free options (soya, coconut and almond) milk versions use products that are sourced from Fairtrade farmers. so you can enjoy your beverage on your way to work knowing the farmers behind the scene have been fairly paid for their contribution to this amazing beverage.
